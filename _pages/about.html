Brief History

My interest in graphic design, animation and coding began as a teenager. Since the beginning I have been interested in art and technology, which pulled me into this field. I studied graphic and web design at John Abbott College (graduating on the dean’s list) and began taking on my first small freelance projects on the side. After graduation I started working full time as an in-house graphic designer at restaurant franchising company Foodtastic, where I had the opportunity to work with a wide variety of different brand and marketing campaigns. Using design solutions for packaging, print and web design I create compelling visuals with the goal of increasing brand recognition and boosting sales and conversions.

I now have over 5 years of experience working and studying in the graphic and web design field, and am continuing to grow experience my skillset.
